# No Way Down

## Basic Concept of the Game
In these Game the Player have ability to move Forward, Left and Right, but it cannot Move backward, which is his disability.. \
Player is trapped on a Maze , and Player have to esacpe from it to Save it's life\
To do so, the Player have to cross some different levels in the maze
